My sister school-campas

My sister school is a Central Board of Secondary Education school located in Rajasthan, Sri ganganagar, India. Sri ganganagar, Rajasthan, India. The pre-primary and early years learning programme at CPS is a system where the tenets of Montessori education are scientifically combined with tried and tested teaching methods, focussing on language development and logical thinking, for children to start their first steps into the education system.

ART CLASSROOM

To kindle a conducive learning environment on par with the best institutions worldwide, My sister school has spared no effort or investment to ensure that the best infrastructure is made available to students to enable them to pursue their education with ease and comfort.

Sports & games

Students are segregated into four houses namely Alphard House, Canopus House, Diadem House and Plasma House. Intra-mural games and sports are conducted to promote healthy competitions among the four houses where the adrenaline is gushed up as they take part in all events energetically.
